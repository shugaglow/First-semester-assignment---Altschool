Why I Joined Altschool Engineering Program

My passion for technology and design has always driven me to understand and create user-friendly digital experiences as a product designer.
This interest led me to pursue a Frontend Engineering program at Altschool, where I am excited to develop my skills and turn my passion into a career.

Being a returning student from the product design track, Altschool attracted me with its innovative curriculum and project-based learning approach.
The school's emphasis on real-world applications ensures that I will gain practical, hands-on experience.
The dynamic and collaborative learning environment at Altschool also promises to provide me with the tools and support necessary to excel in the fast-evolving tech industry.

My Goals for Enrolling in the Frontend Engineering Program

Master Frontend Technologies

My primary goal is to become proficient in the essential frontend technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React and Angular. I aim to build a solid foundation in these technologies to create responsive, interactive, and visually appealing web applications.

Develop Problem-Solving Skills

I want to enhance my problem-solving skills by working on diverse projects and tackling real-world challenges. Understanding how to debug and optimize code, as well as how to improve user experience, will be crucial in my journey as a frontend engineer.

Build a Strong Portfolio

I am eager to collaborate with fellow students and learn from experienced mentors. Sharing knowledge, receiving feedback, and participating in team projects will help me grow both personally and professionally.

Collaborate with Peers and Mentors

By the end of the program, I aspire to have a portfolio of well-crafted projects that showcase my skills and creativity. These projects will demonstrate my ability to design and develop user-centric web applications, which will be essential when seeking job opportunities.

Stay Updated with Industry Trends

The tech industry is constantly evolving, and I want to stay current with the latest trends and best practices in frontend development. This includes understanding new tools, frameworks, and methodologies that can improve my efficiency and the quality of my work.

Prepare for a Career in Tech

Ultimately, my goal is to prepare for a successful career in tech. I want to leverage the skills and knowledge gained at Altschool to secure a position as a frontend engineer in a dynamic and innovative company. I aspire to contribute to meaningful projects that enhance user experiences and solve real-world problems
